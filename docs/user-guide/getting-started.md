# Getting Started with EFT Quest Tracker

Welcome to EFT Quest Tracker! This guide will help you start tracking your Escape from Tarkov quest progress in just a few minutes.

## What is EFT Quest Tracker?

EFT Quest Tracker is a web-based tool that helps you track your quest progress in Escape from Tarkov. It provides:

- Visual quest dependency trees showing prerequisites and unlock paths
- Progress tracking that automatically unlocks dependent quests
- Filters for traders, maps, quest types, and your PMC level
- Kappa container quest highlighting
- Guest mode for browsing without an account

## Quick Start (Guest Mode)

You can browse all quests without creating an account:

1. Visit [https://learntotarkov.com](https://learntotarkov.com)
2. Click **Browse Quests**
3. Start exploring the quest tree

**Note:** Progress won't be saved without an account.

## Creating Your Account

To save your progress across sessions and devices:

1. Visit [https://learntotarkov.com](https://learntotarkov.com)
2. Click **Create Account** or **Sign Up Free**
3. Choose a sign-up method:
   - **Email & Password** - Traditional registration
   - **Google** - Sign in with your Google account
   - **Discord** - Sign in with your Discord account
4. Complete the CAPTCHA verification
5. You're ready to track quests!

## Your First Quest

After logging in, you'll see the quest tracker with a welcome dialog explaining key features. Here's how to mark your first quest as complete:

1. Find a quest you've completed in the **Available** section
2. **Click** the quest card to mark it as complete
3. Watch as dependent quests automatically unlock
4. See your progress percentage update in the top bar

That's it! Your progress is automatically synced and saved.

## Understanding the Interface

### Top Navigation Bar

- **EFT Quest Tracker** - Click to return home
- **Quests** - Main quest tracking page
- **Progress Stats** - Shows completed/in-progress/available quest counts
- **Profile Menu** - Access settings and sign out

### Quest Status Types

The tracker categorizes quests into three states:

- **Available** (Green) - Prerequisites completed, ready to start
- **In Progress** (Yellow) - Started but not completed
- **Completed** (Gray) - Finished

### Filter Bar

Use filters to focus on specific quests:

- **Search** - Find quests by name (press `/` to focus)
- **Traders** - Filter by Prapor, Therapist, Skier, etc.
- **Status** - Show only Available/In Progress/Completed
- **Maps** - Filter by location (Customs, Woods, etc.)
- **Types** - Filter by quest type (Elimination, Exploration, etc.)

### PMC Level

Set your current PMC level to:

1. Hide quests above your level (keeps the view clean)
2. Highlight quests you can actually accept in-game

Click **Show All** to reveal hidden quests temporarily.

### Progress Indicators

- **Top Bar** - Shows overall completion percentage with breakdown
- **Quest Cards** - Display trader, level requirement, and map
- **Kappa Badge** - Indicates quests required for Kappa container
- **Unlock Count** - Shows how many quests will unlock when completed

## Tips for New Users

1. **Set your PMC level first** - This filters out irrelevant high-level quests
2. **Focus on "Next Up" quests** - These unlock the most dependent quests
3. **Use Kappa filter** - If you're pursuing Kappa, toggle this to prioritize
4. **Try keyboard shortcuts** - Press `?` to see all shortcuts
5. **Use focus mode** - Double-click a quest to see its entire dependency chain
